# ADR-0007: Mandatory Self-Hosted Fallback for Every Managed Service
**Status:** Final | **Implements:** RFC-0013 | **Volume:** 11

## Context
Constitution Principle 9 (No Vendor Lock-in) requires portability; managed-service
convenience can silently erode this over time if not actively checked.

## Decision
Volume 11's managed-service table (Ch. 2) must list a working self-hosted fallback for
every entry. Volume 14 adds a periodic CI check that the self-hosted `docker-compose`
path still works.

## Consequences
- Some managed-service-only conveniences are explicitly out of bounds.
- Ongoing CI cost to maintain the portability check, accepted as the enforcement
  mechanism for this principle.
