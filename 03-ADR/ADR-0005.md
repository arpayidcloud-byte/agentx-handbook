# ADR-0005: Conservative Destructive Classification for fs.write
**Status:** Final | **Implements:** RFC-0006 | **Volume:** 7

## Context
Precisely distinguishing "new file write" from "overwrite" is more complex to implement
correctly than a blanket rule, and a misclassification here risks silent data loss.

## Decision
v0.1 classifies all `fs.write` calls as destructive (approval-gated), regardless of
new-vs-overwrite distinction.

## Consequences
- More approval prompts than strictly necessary early on.
- Zero risk of a false-negative silent overwrite.
- Precise classification is a documented future relaxation, not a permanent limitation.
