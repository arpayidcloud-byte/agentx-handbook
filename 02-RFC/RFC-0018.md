# RFC-0018: Contract Test Template Format
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 14 (Testing & QA Strategy)

## Problem
Constitution Principle 6 (Testable by Default) needs a concrete, checkable definition —
otherwise "testable" is unenforceable at Volume-approval time.

## Proposal
A fixed template format at `08-Examples/<volume-slug>/contract.test.ts` per Volume 14,
Ch. 1, required before that Volume can be marked `Approved — Implementation-Gated`
(see ADR-0009). A Volume may reach `Approved — Architecture` without this template.

## Alternatives
- Leave test format to implementer discretion — rejected, makes Principle 6 unenforceable
  and inconsistent across AI-Studio-generated implementations.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 14, Ch. 1 and FR-1. See ADR-0009.
