# RFC-0008: TaskContext Retrieval Strategy
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 6 (Memory Engine)

## Problem
Full conversation replay on every agent call is cost-unbounded (Volume 4 cost tracking);
vector/semantic search adds infrastructure not yet justified at v0.1 volume.

## Proposal
Last-N results plus a rolling summary (Volume 6, Ch. 2) as the v0.1 middle ground.

## Alternatives
- Full replay — rejected, unbounded cost growth.
- Vector/semantic search — deferred, premature infrastructure for current volume.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 6, Ch. 2. Revisit via new RFC once context-window pressure
is observed.
