# RFC-0012: RBAC Role Model
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 10 (Enterprise Platform)

## Problem
Volume 9 assumes a single operator; multi-tenant orgs need scoped permissions for
multiple members.

## Proposal
Three roles — Owner, Developer, Viewer — with fail-closed permission checks (unrecognized
role = zero permissions).

## Alternatives
- Fine-grained per-permission ACLs from day one — rejected as premature complexity before
  real multi-user usage data exists; can be layered on later without breaking the 3-role
  baseline.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 10, Ch. 2.
