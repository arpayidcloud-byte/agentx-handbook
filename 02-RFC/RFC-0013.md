# RFC-0013: Mandatory Self-Hosted Fallback
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 11 (Cloud Platform)

## Problem
Managed-service convenience can silently become a hard dependency, violating
Constitution Principle 9 (No Vendor Lock-in) over time.

## Proposal
Every managed service recommendation in Volume 11 must have a documented, working
self-hosted fallback in the same table; Volume 14 adds a periodic CI check verifying
`docker-compose up` still brings up a fully working self-hosted stack.

## Alternatives
- Allow managed-only conveniences for faster initial delivery — rejected, directly
  contradicts a named Constitution principle.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 11, Ch. 2 and Volume 14, Ch. 4. See ADR-0007.
