# RFC-0010: No Timeout-Based Auto-Approval
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 9 (CLI Platform)

## Problem
A paused, awaiting-approval task graph with no operator present could either stall
indefinitely or need an automatic resolution after a timeout.

## Proposal
No timeout-based auto-approve or auto-reject in v0.1; graphs stay paused until an explicit
`agentx approve`/`reject` command, with `status` surfacing pending approvals prominently.

## Alternatives
- Configurable timeout with auto-approve — rejected, violates Constitution Principle 7's
  intent (silent auto-approval of a destructive action).
- Auto-reject on timeout — rejected, silently stalls legitimate work.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 9, Ch. 2 and Section 10.
