# RFC-0015: AI Company OS Composition-Only Constraint
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 12 (AI Company OS)

## Problem
An org-level coordination layer risks duplicating Core Runtime's Scheduler or Workflow
Engine's TaskGraph logic at a second layer, undermining Constitution Principle 10.

## Proposal
Volume 12 may only compose existing `TaskGraph`/Scheduler instances (visibility,
prioritization, budget) — any apparent need for a new execution primitive must be
redirected to an RFC against Volume 2 or Volume 5 instead.

## Alternatives
- A richer org-level execution engine — rejected, violates Small Stable Core even at the
  top of the Volume stack.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 12, Ch. 1 and FR-1/NFR-1.
