# RFC-0004: Decomposition Output Validation
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 3 (Agent Platform)

## Problem
LLM-driven decomposition can produce malformed or cyclic task graphs.

## Proposal
`LlmDecomposer` output is schema-validated before being returned to Core Runtime;
invalid output is a retryable failure. Volume 5's `buildGraph` additionally rejects
cycles before scheduling.

## Alternatives
- Trust model output directly — rejected, no safety net against hallucinated structure.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 3, Ch. 4 and Volume 5, Ch. 1.
