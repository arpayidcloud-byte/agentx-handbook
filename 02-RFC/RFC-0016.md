# RFC-0016: Org Budget Enforcement Mode
**Status:** Accepted | **Volume:** 12 (AI Company OS)

## Problem
Hard budget cutoffs risk blocking legitimate work with no real-world usage data to
calibrate ceilings correctly.

## Proposal
Advisory enforcement: new graph submissions are queued with a warning as spend approaches
the ceiling, not silently blocked, consistent with the handbook's general
advisory-before-enforcement bias.

## Alternatives
- Hard cutoff at ceiling — rejected for v1 of this Volume; revisit once real spend
  patterns are observed.

## Decision
Adopted as specified in Volume 12, Ch. 2.
